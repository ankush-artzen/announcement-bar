Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Countdown Banner App ("the Service").

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary for functionality, including:

  • Email address (if applicable)
  • Banner preferences (stored locally or in database)
  • Payment and subscription status via Stripe

2. How We Use Information

We use your information to:

  • Personalize and display banners
  • Enable premium features for paid users

3. Payment Processing

All payments are handled securely via Stripe. We do not store your card or payment details directly. Stripe may collect and store billing information in accordance with their own Privacy Policy.

4. Cookies & Local Storage

We may use your browser's localStorage or sessionStorage to:

  • Track banner view limits
  • Save user preferences and banner settings
  • Improve user experience
You may clear this data through your browser settings.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your information with third parties, except trusted services required to operate this app, such as Stripe for payment processing.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request deletion of your data
